About us

A small team that cares.

We're a senior crew of designers and engineers. No layers, no middlemen. You talk to the people actually doing the work, start to finish.

How we got here

summit studios started for a pretty simple reason. We kept seeing good ideas get buried under software that was slow, confusing, or held together with tape. We figured we could do better, so we did.

These days we work with founders getting their first version off the ground and with bigger teams who need an extra set of hands they can trust. The work changes, but the way we approach it doesn't: understand the problem properly, keep things simple, and build something that lasts longer than the launch.

We stay small on purpose. It keeps us close to the work and honest about what we take on.

Straight talk

We'll tell you what we really think, even when it's not what you hoped to hear. It saves everyone time.

Craft over flash

We care about the boring stuff that makes software feel good: speed, clarity, and details that hold up.

In it with you

We treat your product like it's ours. That means we sweat the launch and stick around after it.
